Persons of Interest

This is a list of most of the important characters in the game. (not every character)

  • Teimo Pielinen

    • Teimo is the owner of Teimon kauppa (Teimo's shop) and Pub Nappo. He is perhaps the most important of the characters, as he sells food, drinks, fuel, mosquito spray, and some car components. He can also ask the player if they're interested in delivering some advertisements for him.


  • Leif Fleetari

    • Leif is the owner of Fleetarin korjaamo & maalaamo (Fleetari Repair Shop). About as important as Teimo, he is able to repair, tune, and respray your car, as well as fit tires to rims. He also organizes the rally, pays for car wrecks, and offers a job requiring the player to dump sewage on the property of Lindell.


  • Lindell

    • Lindell is the owner of Katsastus Lindell Oy (Lindell Inspection Shop). Also important, he "judges" your car with his magic screwdriver and, if there are no problems, issues your car registration plates, which are required to drive on the highway. Does not offer refunds for failed inspections. Needs to be visited once every four ingame weeks for further inspection.


  • Jasu K채rl채

    • A local amis (youngster), he hangs out ocassionally in front of Teimo's shop with his girlfriend, Suski. He is hostile to the player, making insulting remarks when they see him, ranging from their vehicle to their choice of drinks. Can also knock out the player if hit.


  • Toivo Kesseli

    • The uncle of the player, he owns the van and septic tanker. After the player progresses, he will lend the van and tanker, and will eventually lose his license due to speeding, giving the player his vehicles permanently. Afterwards, he can be found in front of his house, on a lawn chair, sharing his "wisdoms", should the player wish to listen to him.


  • Sirkka Kesseli

    • The grandmother of the player, she regularly calls the player to ask for fish or groceries. She tends to ramble, repeating several subjects in the same conversation. Pays a lot of money when her requests are fulfilled.


  • Pena Kesseli

    • The player's cousin. Pena is a drunk driver that circles the map indefinitely in his green Fiat. If the player is on foot, he will stop and offer them a ride back home. Otherwise, if the player is inside a vehicle, he will keep driving at the same speed, making no effort to avoid you, leading to a crash if you don't react. Hated by the community for this.


  • Ritoranta

    • The owner of the lakeside shack. Plays ventti (blackjack), and keeps raising the stakes to, eventually, their house and car (and also the player's). When defeated, he flips the ventti table, and leaves his house on all four legs, like a pig.


  • Jouko Ollevi

    • The local drunk. Can call the player at 3AM to ask to be driven home, and can buy kilju (homebrewn alcoholic drink). Will get angry if scammed into buying water. Also has a suitcase full of lottery money, which can be stolen, leading to him hanging around a bridge for a long time.


  • Kari L채t채nen

    • The bus driver. Stops in the town and can drive the player to any bus station if they buy a ticket. Useful if stuck in the town with no car.
